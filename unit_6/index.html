About Us

Aloha! Welcome to Golden Pineapple Bakeshop. Whether you're looking for a quick doughnut or pastry for breakfast, or a curry croquette for lunch, we have just what you are craving.

We offer cakes for all occasions, doughnuts, croissants, cookies, a variety of Hawaiian and Filipino breads, and so much more. Come on by and take a look inside, you'll find some good old fashioned treats, as well as some goodies from across Asia and the pacific.
