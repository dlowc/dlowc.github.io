About Us

Aloha! Welcome to Golden Pineapple Bakery. Whether you're looking for a quick bun for breakfast, or a curry croquette for lunch, we have just what you are craving.

We offer cakes for all occasions, malasadas, savory and sweet buns, manapua or baos, a variety of breads, and so much more. Come on by and take a look inside, you'll find some good old fashioned treats from across Asia and the pacific.
